Chef Dan Bark

A Korean American born, Chef Dan Bark dedicated nearly 20 years to his craft working in the 2-Michelin Star restaurant, Avenues, and as sous chef for the famed 3-Michelin Star, Grace. In 2015, on a new journey, he moved to Thailand and in 2017, opened his dream restaurant.
