Chef Dan Bark

A Korean American born, Chef Dan Bark dedicated nearly 20 years to his craft working in the 2-Michelin Star restaurant, Avenues, and as sous chef for the famed 3-Michelin Star, Grace. In 2015, on a new journey, he moved to Thailand and in 2017, opened his dream restaurant.

Our Kitchen

In the middle, there is a large stainless-steel work table where many dishes can be prepared at the same time. Around the sides, there are stainless-steel counters that are easy to clean and keep hygienic. On the wall, you can see big ovens, stoves, and an exhaust hood to remove smoke and steam. Plates, pots, and cooking tools are organized neatly under the tables and in cabinets, so chefs can grab them quickly. On the right side, vegetables and other ingredients are already prepared for fast cooking.
